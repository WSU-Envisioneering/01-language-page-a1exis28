UX Designers

UX designers work on digital products. They mostly work on the user experience aspect of the product. Although products are different, the design process seems to stay the same. There is an essential vocabulary of UX design. Here are some of the basic thing you should know!

  • A/B testing
  • Accessibility
  • Card Sort
  • End User
  • Information Architecture
  • Prototype
  • User Flow
  • Wireframe
  • Mockup
  • Human Computer Interaction
