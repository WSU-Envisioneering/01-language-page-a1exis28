UX & UI Designers

UX and UI designers work together on the visually appealing aspect of digital products. UX is for "User eXperience". UI is for "User Interface". UX designers make the digital products usable, enjoyable, and accessible while the UI designers create visual and interactive elements for apps, websites, and devices. Together, these two in the end make an amazing digital product which the users of the product will be pleased with.
